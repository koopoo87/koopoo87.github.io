Brain Composer

This project exlores possibility of the brain interface in musical application
The short musical melody extracted from human brain signal triggers AI-Engine that has been trained to produce music in the sytle of Bach (Music Style Transfer). The generated music then transmit the signal to the punching machine that produces 30 note music strip.
