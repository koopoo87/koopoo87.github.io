Design x Technology x Humanity

When one places a support at the center of a circular disk, the disk wobbles and rotates but always stays supported at the middle.
Similarly, I want to be a designer that, like the disk, reaches and combines ideas from different fields like
design, technology, and humanity, and is always supported by my firm philosophy that sustains myself.

Minjoo Cho

About Me

Minjoo Cho is a creative interaction technologist at Indeed Innovation based in Hamburg, Germany.

She has a Masters degree in myDesignLab at KAIST (Korean Advanced Institute of Science and Technology)

Prior to KAIST, she worked at Samsung C-Lab (Creative Lab) & Mobile Product Planning team at Samsung Electronics, HQ.

Before working at Samsung, she either studied and worked in variety of fields spanning a broad range.
