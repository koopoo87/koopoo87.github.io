Project Management at Samsung Mobile

About the Team

To broaden my perspective to see "the forest", I started my first work career at Global Project Management Group in Samsung Electronics. As a project manager of the cutting edge Samsung Tablets, I took in charge of the projects from their product planning stage to their the end of production.



Projects

Followings are the projects I participated as a product manager in Samsung Mobile

Samsung Galaxy Note 10.1

Samsung Galaxy Note 10.1 was the first table project that tried to combine the advantage of the note taking device( Galaxy Note Series ) and that of the big sized tablet models. As Galaxy Note 10.1 was the initiative product embedding the note taking fuction in the table category, I needed to hold numberless workshops to introduce the key selling points of the tablets and to demonstrate how to use the function to the all Samsung Global Officies around the world. The product successfully launched in world-wide in 2011 and it paved the way to Samsung’s tablets to take a large portion of pies within the table that was orginally predominated by Apple’s iPad.
