Samsung C-LAB





About the Team

Employees at Samsung's Creative Lab (C-Lab)are encouraged to pitch ideas with creative or altruistic elements. The winners are given time off from their the current works and are invited to create their ideas in reality.
After few months of the screening process, I was selected as one of 15 members who were given honor to work in the C-lab HQ for a year.





Music for the deaf

In 2013, Samsung was known to be reached its pinnacle of success after the consecutive earning shock with Galaxy series. Especially, Samsung’s new strategy as a “Life Companion” was known to be one of the success points because it softened soften its brand image to be more human-friendly.

Though with such efforts however, I thought Samsung was not caring enough for the people being alienated from the main benefits that Sam- sung smartphone provides; make and receive calls.

What is music to the deaf?



Concept

The concept of the product was to combining two other senses (Visual/Touch) to expand sound experience

Design

Our Deaf Music System consisted of the Android Application providing interactive typography of the music video on market and the wearable device expanding their music experience with the device in the similar design to the existing headset considering the characteristics of the deaf wanting to hide their disability.



User Study

ASeveral user interviews were conducted to test with deaf users invited from Seoul Deaf Association. Our main purpose was to observe how much they are interested in listening or enjoying the music and how they actually do to enjoy it.
